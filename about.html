About Advocate Anoop Pareek

Advocate Anoop Pareek is a Jaipur-based legal practitioner representing clients before the Rajasthan High Court (Jaipur Bench), district courts, and related forums. He is known for practical legal strategy, disciplined case preparation, and clear client communication.

With more than 25 years of legal practice, he has advised individuals, families, and businesses on litigation and preventive legal planning across contested and advisory matters.
